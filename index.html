NTU Open Source Society

NTUOSS is NTU's resident hacker club! We hold technical workshops every Friday (aptly named TGIFHacks) which are completely open to the public and usually happen at this venue. Do like our Facebook page at fb.com/ntuoss to be updated on our events. We're also holding our hackathon - iNTUition - this weekend, which is open to all students!
